How to Get the Most From This Guide
Do not try to use all 300 prompts at once.
Pick the category most relevant to what you are working on right now. Find 5 prompts that solve your most immediate problems. Use them this week and refine them based on what comes back.
The prompts with brackets are templates. Fill in the brackets with your specific context. The more specific you are, the better Claude performs.
Every prompt in this guide replaces between 30 minutes and several hours of manual work. Use them consistently and the compounding effect on your output will be significant.
